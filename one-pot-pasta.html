One-Pot Pasta
Italian; 30 Minutes; ~$25

This is one of the most versatile recipes I make. The only required ingredients are pasta and sauce. Everything else can be changed to suit your dietary needs. I personally will usually add some form of protein, a few different veggies, and some cheese.

Ingredient List

  • 1 14.5 oz Box Pasta
  • 1 to 2 lbs Protein (Your Choice)
  • Italian Seasoning if Protein is unseasoned
  • Olive Oil for Sautéing
  • 1 Jar Pasta Sauce (Your Choice)
  • 1 16 oz Tub Low-Fat Cottage Cheese
  • 1 Zucchini, diced
  • 1 Yellow Onion, diced
  • 1 to 2 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 Yellow Bell Pepper, diced
  • 8 oz Sliced White Mushrooms
  • Mozzarella or Italian Cheese Blend for topping

Directions

  1. Wash and dice your onion, bell pepper, and zucchini
  2. Cut up your protein to the size you prefer
  3. Blend together your sauce and cottage cheese
  4. In a large skillet or pot, add olive oil and heat on medium
  5. Add the onion, bell pepper, zucchini, and mushrooms to the pot
  6. Cook until the onion becomes translucent and softens
  7. Add the garlic and cook for another minute or so
  8. Remove the vegetables from the pot
  9. Add more olive oil if needed, then add your protein and seasoning to the pot
  10. Cook protien completely, stirring occasionally
  11. Once protein is cooked, add veggies, sauce, and noodles to the pot
  12. Cover with a lid and cook for time specified by noodles, stirring occasionally
  13. Add your cheese mix at the end and stir in
  14. Enjoy!
