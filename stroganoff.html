Nana Kay's Stroganoff
Nana Kay Recipe; 2 to 3 Hours; ~$35

This recipe is a staple of Nana Kay's Sunday Dinners. It does take longer than most of my other recipes, but if you've got the time, it's definitely worth the wait!

Ingredient List

  • 1 14.5 ox Box Pasta, I used Protein Penne
  • 1.5 oz Packet Beef Stroganoff Mix
  • 1 Tbsp Beef Base
  • 2 Cups Hot Water
  • 1 to 2 Cups Sour Cream
  • 8 oz Sliced White Mushrooms
  • 1 to 2 lbs Petite Sirloin
  • 1 to 2 Tbsp All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Yellow Onion, diced

Directions

  1. Dice your onion and set aside
  2. Slice your petite sirloin into strips, about 1 inch thick
  3. Add your flour and sirloin strips to a Ziploc and shake to cover
  4. Combine hot water and beef base until base disolves
  5. In a large skillet, sauté your onions and mushrooms until onions become translucent
  6. Remove the onions and mushrooms from the pan
  7. Brown the meat in some of the beef base mix, about 1/2 a Cup
  8. Add back the veggies, the remaining beef base mix, and the stroganoff mix, then stir to combine
  9. Cook on low with a medium simmer for 2 to 3 hours, stirring every 10 minutes or so
  10. Be sure to scrape the bottom of the pan when you stir to avoid sticking
  11. Bring a large pot of water to a boil for the noodles when your stroganoff gets close to done
  12. When the meat is fully tender and you're ready to serve, add in your sour cream and stir together
  13. For serving, you can combine the noodles and sauce into one big mix, or you can ladle the sauce independently
  14. Enjoy!
