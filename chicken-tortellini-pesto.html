Chicken Tortellini Pesto
Italian; 30 Minutes; ~$50 (Double Batch)

This recipe is a great balance between healthy and tasty! Great protein from the tortellini and chicken, and lots of nutrients from the spinach, tomatoes, and pesto!

Ingredient List

  • 2 20 oz Bags Tortellini, I used Spinach and Cheese
  • 22 oz Jar Basil Pesto
  • 2 lbs Pulled Rotisserie Chicken
  • 10 oz Grape Tomatoes, halved
  • 10 oz Bag Spinach, washed and cut up
  • 1 to 2 Cups Italian Cheese Mix

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
  2. While the water is heating up, cut your chicken if the pieces are too big
  3. Cut your tomatoes in half
  4. Chop your spinach as fine as you prefer, you can use a food processor for very fine
  5. In a skillet, combine your sauce, veggies, and chicken and turn on medium heat
  6. Cook your tortellini as instructed on the package
  7. Drain your tortellini, optionally spraying down with cold water if planning to refrigerate immediately
  8. Add your noodles to the skillet, along with the cheese
  9. Stir to combine
  10. If the mixture is too watery, turn up heat and boil off some, stirring frequently
  11. Enjoy!
