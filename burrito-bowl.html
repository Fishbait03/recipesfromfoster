Burrito Bowls
Hispanic; 30 Minutes; ~$35

This recipe is great to make a bunch of because it reheats great. You can eat it just as a bowl, with chips, or put it in a tortilla and make it a real burrito!

Ingredient List

  • 1 Bell Pepper, diced
  • 1 to 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 to 2 lbs Chicken Breast, cubed
  • 10 oz Can Rotel Diced Tomatoes in Green Chiles
  • 15 oz Can Black Beans, drained and rinsed
  • 8 oz Can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 oz Packet Taco Seasoning
  • 1.5 Cups Chicken Stock
  • 1 Cup White Rice, washed
  • Mexican Cheese Mix, for topping

Directions

  1. Dice your bell pepper
  2. Cube your chicken breast, trying to get similar sized cubes
  3. Open your 3 cans
  4. Measure out 1.5 cups of chicken stock or 1.5 cups hot water with 1.5 tsp of chicken bouillon mixed in
  5. Measure out your rice
  6. In a large skillet, add your olive oil and heat on medium heat
  7. Add your chicken breast and cook thoroughly
  8. Once your chicken is fully cooked, add all remaining ingredients to the pan and stir to combine
  9. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and cover
  10. Let cook for 15 to 20 minutes, stirring halfway through
  11. After the 15 or 20 minutes, you can add your Mexican Cheese to mix in or leave it to be a garnish
  12. Top with guacamole, sour cream, salsa and cheese (if left out originally)
  13. Enjoy!
